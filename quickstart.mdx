WORM VEIL AI drops you straight into a private, uncensored AI chat session the moment you open the app. There are no accounts to create, no emails to verify, and no personal information to hand over — your anonymous identity is generated automatically and stored only in your browser. Follow the steps below to go from zero to your first secure conversation in minutes.

<Steps>

  <Step title="Open WORM VEIL AI">
    Navigate to the WORM VEIL AI web app in Chrome, Edge, or Safari. The platform runs entirely in your browser — no installation required.

    On your very first visit, the app silently registers an anonymous account for you. A unique user ID is generated and saved to your browser's `localStorage`. This ID is the only identifier the system ever associates with your session, and it never leaves your device in a form tied to personal information.

    <Tip>
      Copy your unique user ID from the sidebar (open the hamburger menu → **Chats** tab → scroll to the footer) and store it somewhere safe if you want to restore access to your chat history from another device or browser. Without it, cross-device continuity is not possible.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose an Agent Mode">
    Locate the **agent selector dropdown** in the top bar — it defaults to **🤖 Normal**. Click it to reveal the six available modes and select the one that best matches your task:

    | Mode | Best for |
    |---|---|
    | **Normal** | General-purpose conversation and everyday questions |
    | **Pentester** | Penetration testing methodology, CTF challenges, and security assessments |
    | **OSINT** | Open-source intelligence gathering, reconnaissance, and data correlation |
    | **Exploit Dev** | Vulnerability research, shellcode, and low-level exploit development |
    | **Social Engineering** | Phishing simulations, pretexting scenarios, and awareness training |
    | **Code Review** | Auditing source code for bugs, logic flaws, and security weaknesses |

    Each mode tunes the underlying Dolphin 3.0 Abliterated 8B model's system prompt and response style for its specialist domain. You can switch modes at any point mid-session — the change takes effect on your very next message.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Your First Chat">
    Click inside the **textarea** at the bottom of the chat window and type your message. When you're ready to send:

    - Press **Enter** to send the message immediately.
    - Press **Shift + Enter** to insert a newline and keep composing.
    - Or click the **Send** button to the right of the textarea.

    Watch the **status badge** in the top bar — it moves through three states:

    - 🟢 **ONLINE** — The backend is ready and waiting.
    - 🟡 **GENERATING** — The model is actively streaming a response to you.
    - 🔴 **OFFLINE** — The AI backend is unreachable.

    <Note>
      If the status badge turns red and displays **OFFLINE**, the model backend is temporarily unavailable. This can happen during node maintenance or a Tor circuit reset. Wait a moment and refresh the page — the app will automatically attempt to reconnect through one of the three server nodes (Amsterdam, Singapore, or Panama).
    </Note>

    Your conversation is stored only in your browser's `localStorage`. Nothing is written to a server database — all messages are processed in RAM and discarded immediately after the response is delivered.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use Voice Input">
    Click the **microphone button** (🎤) to the right of the Send button to dictate your message instead of typing.

    The Web Speech API transcribes your speech in real time and populates the textarea. Review the transcription, then press **Enter** or click **Send** as usual.

    <Note>
      Voice input is supported in **Chrome**, **Edge**, and **Safari**. Firefox does not currently implement the Web Speech API. If the microphone button is greyed out, check that your browser has been granted microphone permission for the site.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the Scanner & OSINT Panel">
    Click the **🔍 icon** in the top-right corner of the top bar to open the integrated **Scanner & OSINT** panel.

    From this panel you can:

    - Run a **vulnerability scan** against a target host or URL to surface common misconfigurations and known CVEs.
    - Perform an **OSINT lookup** to aggregate publicly available information about a domain, IP address, or identifier.

    Results appear inline within the panel and can be copied directly into the chat window for the active agent to analyse and act on.

    <Tip>
      Switch to **OSINT mode** or **Pentester mode** before pasting scanner results into the chat — these agents are tuned to interpret technical output and suggest concrete next steps.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage Your Privacy">
    WORM VEIL AI never stores your messages server-side, but your chat history does accumulate in `localStorage` on your device. Use the **auto-clear controls** in the sidebar footer (open the sidebar → **Chats** tab → scroll to the bottom) to schedule automatic deletion:

    - **5 minutes** — Clears history five minutes after your last message.
    - **1 hour** — Purges history after an hour of inactivity.
    - **Custom** — Set any interval in minutes that suits your workflow.
    - **Instant** — Wipes all chat history from `localStorage` immediately.

    <Warning>
      Clearing your chat history is irreversible. Because WORM VEIL AI maintains zero server-side logs, there is no backup copy to recover from. Enable auto-clear only when you are certain you no longer need the local record.
    </Warning>

    Your traffic is already routed through Tor and proxy chains by default — you do not need to configure anything to benefit from multi-layer anonymity. See [Anonymity & Privacy](/concepts/anonymity) for a deep dive into the routing architecture.
  </Step>

</Steps>
