## How It Works

WORM VEIL AI routes your messages through a privacy-preserving infrastructure before they ever reach the AI model. Your IP is never stored, and your messages are discarded from memory as soon as a response is generated.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Navigate to WORM VEIL AI in your browser. Your anonymous user ID is created automatically — no email, phone number, or personal information required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an agent mode">
    Select from Normal, Pentester, OSINT, Exploit Dev, Social Engineering, or Code Review to tailor the AI's behavior to your task.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Type your question or use the voice input button. Your message is encrypted in transit and processed by the Dolphin 3.0 Abliterated 8B model with no content restrictions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep your history private">
    Chat history is stored only in your browser's local storage. Use the auto-clear feature to wipe everything on a timer — or manually delete chats at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
